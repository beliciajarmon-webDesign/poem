Willow Tree

deep in the dark of the forest
there lies a willow tree
far from the rays of the sun
and the quiet buzz of the bees

all who journey to see it
are filled with so much glee
as all their sorrows leave them
for the weeping willow tree

generations have revered it
at its sight they take a knee
for they know the source of their joy
is the weeping willow tree

the people return to the sunlight
light it will never see
for it remains in the forest
the weeping willow tree
